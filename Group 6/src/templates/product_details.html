About the NOVA Classification

The NOVA food classification system categorizes foods according to the extent and purpose of industrial processing:

  • Group 1 (Score 1): Unprocessed or minimally processed foods
  • Group 2 (Score 2): Processed culinary ingredients
  • Group 3 (Score 3): Processed foods
  • Group 4 (Score 4): Ultra-processed foods

Ultra-processing markers include additives, artificial flavors, colors, and preservatives that suggest industrial manufacturing processes.
